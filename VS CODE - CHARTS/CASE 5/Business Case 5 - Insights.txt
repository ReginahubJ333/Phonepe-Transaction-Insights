Business Case 5 - Insurance Transaction Analysis

1. Total Insurance Transaction By State

Top States

Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu are the highest contributors to insurance transactions.

These states are likely to have higher insurance adoption due to urbanization, financial literacy, and insurance penetration.

Low-Performing States & UTs

Lakshadweep (159), Manipur (405), Ladakh (429), Mizoram (489), Nagaland (568) have very low transaction numbers, showing low penetration.

Other small UTs (e.g., Andaman & Nicobar, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli & Daman & Diu) also contribute minimally.

2. Year-Wise Total Insurance Transactions

Total Transactions Growth: Steady increase from 788K in 2020 → 5.07M in 2024, showing rapid adoption of insurance.

Key Growth Years: Significant jumps in 2021→2022 and 2023→2024, indicating accelerated penetration.

Trend Insight: Clear upward trajectory; reflects rising digital insurance usage and expanding market opportunities.

3. TOP 5 STATES WITH HIGHEST GROWTH IN TRANSACTION IN A YEAR

Lakshadweep and Andaman & Nicobar Islands saw exceptional insurance transaction growth (875% and 689%) — a sign of rapid digital adoption from a low base.Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh also demonstrated strong growth post-2020.Kerala’s 325% surge in 2022 indicates expanding insurance awareness and digital penetration in mature markets.

4. TOTAL INSURANCE AMOUNT OVER YEARS

Strong YoY Increase: Indicates expanding insurance adoption and larger transaction amounts.

Market Insight: Rising trend reflects digital penetration and financial inclusion in India.

5. TOP 5 STATES BY AVERAGE QUARTERLY GROWTH

Karnataka leads with the highest average quarterly insurance transactions (≈1.03 lakh), followed by Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu.The dominance of these states indicates strong insurance adoption and higher digital penetration in southern and western India.
